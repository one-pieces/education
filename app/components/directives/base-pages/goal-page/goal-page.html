Goal

In this lesson we're going to have you master the following target langauge:
   zài … … li / shɑnɡ
   在 … … 里 / 上
   wèi shén me ？ yīn wèi … …
   为 什 么 ？ 因 为 … …
   S+ yào +vp.
   S+ 要 +vp.

By the end of the lesson you will have:
1)    Guessed the meaning from the context.
2)    Correctly pronounced the langauge.
3)    Used the language in real life situations.
